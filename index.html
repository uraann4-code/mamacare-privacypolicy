Legal & Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to MamaCare. We are committed to protecting your privacy and secure health data. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, store, process, and protect your information when you use the MamaCare mobile application.

2. Local Storage First

The primary architectural defense of MamaCare is **local storage**. All logged pregnancy details, medication alarms, symptoms, baby names, and contacts are saved inside a local Room SQLite database located within the secure sandboxed app environment on your physical device. No external entities can read this database.

3. Data Sharing & Sales Protection

We Never Sell Your Data: MamaCare does not and will never sell, lease, rent, or monetize your pregnancy, tracking, symptoms, or private health-related information to any third party, marketing broker, insurance corporation, or advertising network.

4. Data Deletion

You can trigger a full database purge instantly at any time inside the app via Profile > Settings > Privacy and Data > Delete Account. You can also view our Data Deletion Guide. If you uninstall the application, the Android operating system automatically purges your entire sandboxed database and all locally cached logs. We do not retain any shadow records.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions, clarifications, suggestions, or concerns regarding your privacy, data security, or local database mechanics, please write to us directly at dadrehman14@gmail.com.
