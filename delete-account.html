Account Deletion & Purging

We believe you should have complete control over your private health logs. Below is a guide on how to purge your files immediately in the mobile application, or submit an external deletion request.

📱 1. Fast Option: Deletion Inside the App

Since all MamaCare metrics are stored primarily in your local physical hardware database, the fastest way to delete everything is directly in the mobile app. This deletes all database files instantly:

1 Open the MamaCare app

2 Tap on the Profile tab in the bottom bar

3 Tap the Settings gear icon or scroll to Privacy and Data

4 Tap Delete Account & Purge Local Data

*Note: Uninstalling the application also triggers a full operating system purge of your local Room database files.

🚨 What Data Will Be Permanently Deleted?

  • ✔ Profile & Settings: Name, DOB, due date details, gestational configurations, PIN codes.
  • ✔ Health Logs: Symptom logs, blood pressure, glucose records, weight, water tracker, sleep logs.
  • ✔ Medical Documents: Vault metadata, appointment lists, clinic cards, local document references.
  • ✔ Journals & Chats: Personal reflections, moods, AI Assistant text message histories.

Legal Retention Disclaimer: As MamaCare is an offline-first tracker that does not maintain background sync networks, we store no copy of your records in our servers. Once deleted, there is no backup and the data is lost forever.
